Our Goals

Make Renting Popular

Renting textbooks is an obvious but uncommon choice for students who wish to save money on textbooks. We wish to make renting popular among college students. It is a logical and beneficial concept prevalent in Western countries that is yet to set a stronghold in our country.

Help students Save money

Education does not come cheap, nor do textbooks. We intend to provide students with good reasons to opt for our book renting service and thus, help them lead a more economic college life.

Slowly Expand

We currently provide our services only in Kerala University. We're planning on expanding into a few other colleges. Someday we plan to launch an All-India Portal. If you think that you could help us achieve our goals, write to us at operations@textrent.in.
