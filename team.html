Our Team

The Whole Team

We are a group of students just like you. Each one of us is personally committed to TextRent and all of the Company’s goals. We work together to bring you our services and hope to make your life easier.

The Administrators

The Administrators work to coordinate the whole TextRent network and ensure that your orders are well received and recorded. Along with managing the company they are responsible for innovative developments and changes for the greater good of the customers and the company itself. They are acutely aware of the company’s goals and strive to maintain the identity of TextRent.

The Representatives

Our Representatives are friendly and helpful students; he or she is one among you. Our belief in person-to-person delivery prompted us to set up a network with college representatives who can guide you and hand you the books you need in person. In other words, they are essentially the pillars of TextRent. Your college Representatives will always be just a phone call away.
